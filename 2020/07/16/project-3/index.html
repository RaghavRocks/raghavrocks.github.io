In a Nutshell

About

Immunitas is a project by high school students to spread awareness about the amazing superfoods, exercises and lifestyles which can substantially boost your immunity.

  • July 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We began as a school project, and evolved into something more. Raghav, who made the site really loves coding so we decided, a website is good! We posted our videos on youtube and then linked them to the site.

  • Forever

    The Cause

    As we are all aware, being healthy at this point of time is very important, we took our work very seriously knowing that people would be trusting us with their health choices.

  • The Near Future

    What we plan to do...

    As support for this site and team grows, we will expand to actually teaming up with several other NGOs to help provide better nutrition and health to poorer families. Especially those on the harsh streets.

  • Today

    How you can help...

    If a bunch of school students can do this, you can do better. Help us by contributing to your local NGOs and showing them the resources made by us. If somebody provides food to the homeless, make sure the food is nutrient rich and helpful.

Food

You are what you eat. Eating healthier and more nutritious food helps your body produce the things it needs to stay protected against foreign infections. Foods with high amounts of Vitamin C and Iron like Lemon, Ginger, Capsicum and Spinach are good for your immunity.

Meditation

Meditation may not be compulsory but it helps a lot. Put on some nice calm music or nature sounds. Sit down comfortably and take deep breaths, focus on your breathing. You'll feel better when you open your eyes!
